## Description

**Chess that works anywhere — no internet, no account, no ads.**

Kingside is a complete chess companion that lives entirely on your phone. Face a
real engine, sharpen your tactics on thousands of puzzles, and get a full
computer review of every game you play — on a plane, on the subway, anywhere.

**♟ Battle real bots**
Play against genuine Stockfish — the same engine grandmasters train with —
tuned across the full skill range from total beginner to crushing. It runs
on-device, so there's never any lag or "waiting for a server."

**🧩 2,000+ puzzles & a puzzle journey**
Work through a star-gated campaign of 20 themed levels — forks, pins, mates,
sacrifices and more — that unlock as you earn stars. Plus a daily puzzle and
endless rated tactics matched to your level.

**🔍 Unlimited game review**
Every game gets a full move-by-move analysis: accuracy score, blunders and
brilliancies, an evaluation graph, and the engine's best line at each turn —
no paywall, no limits.

**🎙 Play hands-free**
Just say your move — "knight to f3" — and Kingside plays it. Fully hands-free
voice control for when your hands are busy.

**📈 Real rating & progression**
A chess.com-style ELO that rises and falls with your results, a daily-streak
flame to keep you coming back, achievements, and a complete history of your
games to revisit and review.

**👥 Pass & Play**
Hand the phone back and forth for a game with a friend on one device — no
account, no internet.

**Also:** multiple board and piece themes, move takeback, hints, and a clean,
fast, distraction-free design.

Download Kingside and carry a grandmaster-strength opponent, a tactics trainer,
and a game analyst in your pocket — anywhere, fully offline.
