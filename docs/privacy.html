Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 June 2026

The short version: Kingside is a single-player, offline chess app. It does not collect, transmit, or sell your personal data. Everything you do — your games, rating, puzzle progress, and settings — is stored only on your device.

What is stored on your device

  • Your rating, win/loss record, achievements, day-streak, saved and completed games, and app settings. This never leaves your device and is removed if you delete the app or use Settings → Reset App Data.

Microphone & speech recognition (optional)

  • If you use voice moves, the app uses your device's microphone and Apple's on-device speech recognition to turn what you say into a chess move. Audio is processed on-device for that purpose only; it is not stored and not transmitted. You can decline the permission and play normally.

Engine packs (optional)

  • If you install an optional engine pack, the app downloads data only (configuration and neural-net evaluation files) to run on the engine already included in the app. No personal data is sent to download a pack, and packs work fully offline once installed.

Children

  • The app is suitable for ages 4+. It does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes

  • If this policy changes, the "last updated" date above will change.
