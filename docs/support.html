Frequently asked

Is an internet connection required?

No. Kingside plays, reviews games, and saves your progress entirely offline. The only optional time it uses the network is if you choose to download an extra engine pack.

Is this really Stockfish?

Yes — the real Stockfish 18 engine is compiled into the app and runs natively on your device. The difficulty levels adjust its strength, not a different, weaker engine.

How do voice moves work?

Voice moves use your device's microphone and Apple's on-device speech recognition. Audio is processed locally to recognise your move and is never recorded or sent anywhere. You can play without granting microphone access.

How is my rating calculated?

Your rating updates after each rated game using a Glicko-style system, factoring in the opponent's strength and how recently you've played. It's a fun, reasonably accurate estimate of your level versus the bots.

What are downloadable engines?

Optional packs add a neural-net evaluation file (data, not a new program) that runs on the built-in engine. They install from inside the app and work offline afterward.

How do I reset my data?

Go to Settings → Reset App Data in the app. This clears your rating, games, and settings on the device. Deleting the app also removes everything.
