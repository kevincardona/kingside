Why you'll like it

♟️

Genuine Stockfish

Every bot is the real Stockfish 18 engine, compiled into the app and running natively on your device — not a watered-down imitation.

🎯

Calibrated opponents

Pick a rating from Beginner to Max. Bots are tuned to play near their label, so the rating you earn actually means something.

🔍

Accurate game reviews

After each game, get an engine-backed review: accuracy, blunders, and the best moves you missed — analysed move by move.

🎙️

Voice moves

Say your move out loud. On-device speech recognition turns it into a legal move — nothing is recorded or sent anywhere.

🧩

Puzzles & progress

Daily puzzles, a rating that follows you, streaks and achievements — all stored privately on your device.

📦

Downloadable engines

Optionally install extra neural-net evaluation packs to try different engine personalities — data only, fully offline once installed.

Private by design

Kingside doesn't collect, transmit, or sell your personal data. Your games, rating, and settings live only on your device. Read the full Privacy Policy.
